## Known Portability Risks

- The workspace is Windows/PowerShell-specific today, although the materialized package reduces local-path sprawl for reviewer inspection.
- The gem5 binary depends on the repo-local MSYS/UCRT runtime.
- The current shell lacks Git, so commit hashes cannot be used as the local provenance anchor.
- Full-system timing reruns are long and should be grouped into quick audit, medium rerun, and full rerun tiers.
- Vivado evidence is FPGA-oriented and should not be described as ASIC signoff.
- Top-tier submission readiness still depends on broader workload evidence and production-style integration proof.
